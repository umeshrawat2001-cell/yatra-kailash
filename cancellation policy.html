Cancellation Policy

Cancellation / No Show / Early Departure Policy

In the event of cancellation of tour or travel services, whether due to avoidable or unavoidable reasons, Yatra Kailash must be informed in writing. Cancellation charges will be applicable from the date the written request is received and will be applied as follows:

  • days or more prior to arrival: 10% of the total tour/service cost
  • 59 to 30 days prior to arrival: 20% of the total tour/service cost
  • 29 to 15 days prior to arrival: 25% of the total tour/service cost
  • 14 to 8 days prior to arrival: 50% of the total tour/service cost
  • 7 days or less prior to arrival / No Show: No refund

Important Notes

  • Regardless of the above cancellation slabs, a minimum 10% service charge will apply once the booking is confirmed.
  • If a trip is cancelled after commencement, any refund will be limited and subject to recovery from hotels and service providers. No refunds will be provided for unused hotel stays, transportation, meals, or other services.
  • For luxury train journeys such as Palace on Wheels, Royal Rajasthan on Wheels, Deccan Odyssey, Golden Chariot, Indian Maharaja, and Maharajas’ Express, separate cancellation policies apply and will be shared at the time of booking.
  • No refunds will be provided for cancellations during the following periods:
    • High Peak Season (20th December to 15th January)
    • Festival periods (e.g., Diwali, Dussehra, Holi, Pushkar Fair, etc.)
    • Long weekends

Refund Policy

Refunds for unused or unutilized services (cancelled in advance) will be processed as per the cancellation policy mentioned above. The refund will be issued to the original payer.

Please note:
  • The refund process may take 2 to 4 weeks due to banking procedures.
  • Any bank or credit card charges incurred during the refund process will be deducted from the refund amount.

Mandatory Identification Documents

All guests (Foreign Nationals, NRIs, and Indian Nationals) must provide valid photo identification at the time of booking confirmation:

  • Foreign Nationals: Passport copy + valid Visa
  • NRI Clients: Passport copy
  • OCI/PIO Card Holders: Passport copy + OCI/PIO card copy
  • Domestic Clients: Driving License / Voter ID / Passport

Vehicle Usage Policy

Please note that the cost of vehicle usage (cars or coaches with drivers) is limited to the agreed itinerary. Any additional usage beyond transfers or scheduled sightseeing is not included and is generally not permitted unless arranged in advance at an extra cost.
