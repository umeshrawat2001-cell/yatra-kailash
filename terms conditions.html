All correspondence in respect of Tours / Travel Services and bookings should be addressed to Yatra Kailash.

Booking

On confirmation of booking, your contract is with Yatra Kailash. A contract exists when we confirm your tour/travel services and receive the deposit amount.

Payment

A minimum deposit of 20% of the total cost must be paid to hold the booking on a confirmed basis.

Balance Payment

Balance payment must be made 20 days prior to the start date. Bookings within 20 days require full payment at the time of booking.

Special Note

For peak season bookings (Diwali, New Year, Pushkar Fair, etc.) or special train journeys, full payment is required at booking.

Payment Notes
